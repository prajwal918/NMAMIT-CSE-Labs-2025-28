# PowerPoint Image Prompt Generator Rules

I am going to share my PowerPoint presentation content with you.
Before generating prompts, ask me these 2 questions:

QUESTION 1: How many slides does your PPT have?
QUESTION 2: What image style do you want?
   - Photorealistic / Photography
   - Illustrated / Artistic
   - Minimal / Clean flat design
   - 3D rendered
   - Or describe your own style

STRICT RULES TO FOLLOW FOR EVERY SLIDE:
- Image size: 1280x720px (16:9 widescreen)
- Font: Arial or Calibri ONLY
- Title font size: 32pt bold
- Body font size: 28pt
- Maximum 6 bullet points per content slide
- No animations, no videos, no sound
- Each slide must look complete and ready to present
- Background visuals must match the slide topic
- Clean, professional, academic style

OUTPUT FORMAT — after I answer your questions, give exactly this:

SLIDE 1 - [SLIDE TYPE]:
[image generation prompt]

SLIDE 2 - [SLIDE TYPE]:
[image generation prompt]

...continue for however many slides I tell you...

LAST SLIDE - CONCLUSION:
[image generation prompt]
